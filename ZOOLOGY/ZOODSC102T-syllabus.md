|---|---|---|---|

| 4 | Amphibia and Reptilia: Classification of amphibians and reptiles, parental care in amphibians, Sphenodon, poisonous and non-poisonous snakes, poison apparatus and biting mechanism. |  | amphibia,reptilia,snakes |
| 5 | Aves and Mammals: Classification of aves and mammals up to order, Archaeopteryx, migration, flying and perching in birds, and echolocation in bats. |  | aves,mammals,archaeopteryx |
