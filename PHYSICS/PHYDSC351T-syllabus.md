## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Constituents of nucleus and intrinsic properties: mass, radii, charge/matter density, binding energy, packing fraction, binding energy curve, N/Z plot, angular momentum, parity, magnetic moment, dipole and quadrupole moments. Alpha Decay: processes, theory of emission, Gamow's theory, Geiger Nuttall law, alpha-ray spectra, and range determination. | 12 | nucleus-properties,alpha-decay,gamow-theory |
| 2 | Beta decay: energy kinematics, beta spectrum, positron emission, electron capture, neutrino hypothesis, Rein & Cowans experiment. Gamma decay: emission and internal conversion. Nuclear Reactions: types, conservation laws, kinematics, Q-value (alpha, beta, gamma decay), endothermic/exoergic reactions, and scattering cross-section. | 12 | beta-decay,gamma-decay,nuclear-reactions,neutrino |
| 3 | Nuclear Models: Liquid drop model, nuclear fission, Bohr-Wheeler theory, semi-empirical mass formula (significance of terms and stability conditions). Nuclear shell model: assumptions, evidence, and magic numbers. Nuclear force: properties and Meson theory. | 12 | liquid-drop-model,shell-model,nuclear-force,fission |
| 4 | Interaction of Radiation: Cerenkov radiation, Gamma ray interaction (photoelectric effect, Compton scattering, pair production), and Mossbauer effect. Detectors: Ionization chamber, proportional counter, GM Counter, and Cerenkov detector. Accelerators: Cyclotron and Betatron. | 13 | radiation-interaction,detectors,cyclotron,betatron |
| 5 | Particle Physics: Fundamental interactions (strengths and features), particle families, symmetries and conservation laws (Spin, baryon/lepton number, Isospin, Strangeness, hypercharge, charm). Quark model, Standard model, and concepts of charge conjugation, parity, time reversal, and CPT theorem. | 11 | elementary-particles,quark-model,conservation-laws,standard-model |
