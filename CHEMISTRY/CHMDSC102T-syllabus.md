## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Gaseous State I: Kinetic theory of gases, collision frequency, collision diameter, mean free path, viscosity, Maxwell distribution, molecular velocities and equipartition of energy. |  | gases,kinetic-theory,maxwell |
| 2 | Gaseous State II: Real gases, compressibility factor, deviations from ideal behavior, van der Waals equation, critical state, corresponding states, liquefaction and inversion temperature. |  | real-gases,critical-state,vdw |
| 3 | Liquid State: Vapour pressure, surface tension, viscosity, interfacial tension, detergents and temperature variation of viscosity. |  | liquids,surface-tension,viscosity |
| 4 | Solid State: Crystal systems, Bravais lattices, Miller indices, X-ray diffraction, Bragg law, defects, colour centre, band theory and liquid crystals. |  | solid-state,crystals,bragg |
| 5 | Solutions: Ideal and non-ideal solutions, Raoult law, distillation, azeotropes, partial miscibility, steam distillation, Nernst distribution law and solvent extraction. |  | solutions,raoult,azeotropes |
