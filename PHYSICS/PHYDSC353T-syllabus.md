## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Amorphous and Crystalline Materials; Lattice Translation Vectors, Unit Cell, and Lattice with a Basis. Types of Lattices, Bravais lattice, symmetry, point groups, and space groups. Miller Indices, Reciprocal Lattice, and Brillouin Zones. Diffraction of X-rays by Crystals, Bragg's Law, and Theory of Laue Spots. | 11 | crystal-structure,bravais-lattice,braggs-law,miller-indices |
| 2 | Lattice Vibrations and Phonons: Linear Monoatomic and Diatomic Chains; Acoustical and Optical Phonons. Qualitative Description of the Phonon Spectrum, Dulong and Petit's Law, Einstein and Debye theories of specific heat, and the T^3 law. Types of bonding: covalent, Ionic, metallic, Van der Waals, and hydrogen bonds. | 13 | phonons,specific-heat,lattice-dynamics,bonding |
| 3 | Magnetic moment from spin and orbital motion; Effect of magnetic field on angular momentum. Dia, Para, Ferro, and Ferrimagnetic Materials; Classical Langevin Theory and Curie's law. Weiss's Theory of Ferromagnetism and Domains. Analysis of B-H Curve, Hysteresis, and Energy Loss. | 11 | magnetism,bh-curve,hysteresis,langevin-theory |
| 4 | Polarization, Local Electric Field, and Depolarization Field; Electric Susceptibility and Polarizability. Clausius Mosotti Equation and Classical Theory of Electric Polarizability. Normal and Anomalous Dispersion, Lorenz theory, and Cauchy/Sellmeier relations. Raman effect theory. Ferroelectric Properties: Piezoelectric, Pyroelectric, and Ferroelectric effects. | 13 | dielectrics,raman-effect,polarization,piezoelectric |
| 5 | Elementary Band Theory: Free electron theory, Band Gap, Conductors, Semiconductors (P and N type), and insulators. Conductivity, mobility, Hall Effect/coefficient, and Effective mass. Superconductivity: Critical Temperature/field, Meissner effect, and Type I/II superconductors. London's Equation, Penetration Depth, Isotope effect, and BCS theory. | 12 | band-theory,superconductivity,hall-effect,meissner-effect |
