## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Animal Diversity: study of museum specimens from each phylum (at least 3 specimens each) and sub-phyla including specimens of evolutionary significance; key for identification of poisonous and non-poisonous snakes; preparation of an animal album with photographs, cut outs and write-ups on the above taxa. | 15 | animal-diversity,museum-specimens,phylum,snakes,animal-album |
| 2 | Physiology and Biochemistry: preparation of hemin crystals; study of permanent histological sections (slide/micro-photographs) of mammalian pituitary, thyroid, pancreas and adrenal gland; study of permanent slides/micro-photographs of spinal cord, duodenum, liver, lung, kidney, bone and cartilage; study of activity of salivary amylase under optimum conditions. | 15 | physiology,biochemistry,hemin,histological-sections,amylase |
| 3 | Genetics and Evolutionary Biology: study of Mendelian inheritance and gene interactions (Non-Mendelian inheritance) using suitable examples and verification by Chi-square test; study of fossil evidences from plaster cast models or pictures; study of homology and analogy from suitable specimens/pictures. | 15 | genetics,mendelian,non-mendelian,chi-square,fossils,homology,analogy |
| 4 | Molecular Biology: study of Polytene chromosomes from Chironomous/Drosophila larvae; quantitative estimation of DNA using colorimeter or spectrophotometer. | 15 | molecular-biology,polytene-chromosomes,drosophila,dna-estimation |
