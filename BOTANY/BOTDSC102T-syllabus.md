## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Algae: General characteristics, ecology, thallus organization, cell structure, pigments, reserve food, flagella, reproduction, classification systems and role of algae in environment, agriculture, biotechnology and industry. |  | algae,phycology,classification |
| 2 | Divisions of Algae: Characteristics and life cycles of Nostoc, Vaucheria, Chlamydomonas, Volvox, Oedogonium, Coleochaete, Chara, Ectocarpus and Polysiphonia; major algal groups and significance of Prochloron. |  | cyanophyta,chlorophyta,rhodophyta |
| 3 | Fungi and its Associations: General characters of fungi, affinities, thallus organization, cell wall, nutrition, classification, lichens and mycorrhiza. |  | fungi,lichens,mycorrhiza |
| 4 | Major Divisions of Fungi: Life cycles and classification with reference to Synchytrium, Rhizopus, Saccharomyces, Aspergillus, Penicillium, Alternaria, Fusarium, Puccinia, Ustilago, Agaricus, Phytophthora and Albugo; heterokaryosis and parasexuality. |  | mycology,life-cycle,classification |
| 5 | Applied Mycology: Role of fungi in biotechnology, food industry, secondary metabolites, biofertilizers, mycotoxins, biological control, medical mycology and mushroom cultivation. |  | applied-mycology,biotech,mushroom |
